What I do

Design to Code

I specialize in bringing design visions to life by crafting functional and engaging web interfaces. Collaborating with talented UI/UX designers, I meticulously translate their mockups from figma or adobe xd into fluid website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript,Pug and SCSS. My work extends beyond aesthetics; I'm dedicated to creating responsive and user-friendly experiences, ensuring that websites and applications look and perform flawlessly on all devices

Progressive Web App

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) represent the future of web development, and I'm excited to be at the forefront of this transformative technology. PWAs combine the best of both web and mobile applications, offering users a seamless, fast, and reliable experience. My expertise in developing PWAs involves leveraging the latest web technologies like workers and manifest files to create applications that can work offline, load quickly, and provide a native-app-like experience.

Web Accessibility

I believe in creating digital experiences that are inclusive and user-friendly for all. I'm familiar with the best practices for implementing accessible features, such as proper semantic HTML, ARIA roles, and keyboard navigation. I understand the importance of alt text and robust color contrast. By making accessibility a priority in my work, I aim to break down barriers and provide equal access to information and services for everyone.
